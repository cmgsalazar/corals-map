Incidents of illegal coral trade, 2015‑2025

Map shows reported origin countries and transit locations of trafficked corals, where such information is available. Marker size reflects the number of documented cases of illegal coral trade between 2015 to 2025. The map does not represent overall trafficking trends, as the records capture only incidents that were reported and independently verified.

Map: Cherry Salazar for Mongabay • Source: TRAFFIC wildlife trade database
